    Ferme St. Martin

    france locations

    Posted on Jan 2

    This is a classical provencal 22 acre wine olive state on a hill with a breathtakingly view. We booked the full location for our big family reunion in October 2016 (around 15 people). Taking into consideration that the property was shared by four parties, the price of this luxury estate, was absolutely fair (comparing it to similar hotel offerings).

    It’s an outstanding and spacious property of highest quality with a heated pool (very useful in late October holidays) in a lovely environment.
