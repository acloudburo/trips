Küsnacht Scenery Walk #1

Posted on: 25.Feb 2017

One of my favourite walks through the “Küsnachter Tobel” (Tobel = Small canyoin) and Itschnach bypassing some large villas with an interesting architecture and a spectacular view of the Alps.

Distance
6.3 km
Duration
60 - 75 minutes
Minimal Height
321 m
Maximal Height
439 m
Ascent (total)
161 m
Descent (total)
170 m
To get there
Take Bus 912 from Zuerich-Bellevue to Itschnach-Rebweg (around a 19 minutes trip, all 15 or 30 minutes). Online Schedule

The Route on Google Maps


The route will bypass the following attractions.

Rockfall in Küsnachter Tobel

In April 2014, a rock fall of a 250 tons stone took place. The six-meter-high Nagelfluh-Brocken came to a halt near the Dorfbach practically on the footpath. Nobody got hurt.


Big Falls

My favourite “big” falls in the Tobel, are always a fantastic scenery, be it in the summer …


… or winter …


View of the Alps

In the last quarter of the round trip you will get a gorgeous view of the Swiss Alps (if the weather condition permits), with the Zurich See lake in front.
